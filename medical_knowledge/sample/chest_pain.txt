# Chest Pain Assessment Guidelines

## Red Flags
- Pain radiating to arm or jaw
- Associated shortness of breath
- Diaphoresis (sweating)
- Pain at rest

## Initial Assessment
1. Obtain vital signs
2. Perform 12-lead ECG within 10 minutes
3. Obtain cardiac biomarkers (troponin)

## Management
- Aspirin 300mg if ACS suspected
- GTN spray for ongoing chest pain
- Supplemental oxygen if SpO2 < 94%
